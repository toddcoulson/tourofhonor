Veteran Application

The Bucks County Tour of Honor program continues to pay homage to our World War II and terminally ill veterans from all wars. We are also at this time accepting applications from veterans who have served in the Korean War. We view the Tour of Honor trip as a simple acknowledgement, a Thank You for your service and contribution for making the world safe for democracy. Please download the form below, fill it out and return to the address listed on the application.

Guardian Application

Our Tour of Honor would not be possible without the help of our Guardians. Guardians play a significant role on our trip and are responsible for helping to ensure every veteran has a safe and memorable experience. Guardians must be between the ages of 18 and 70 years of age. Spouses of veterans may not serve as guardians.
